Solution 1.3.5.1.

The Euclidean Algorithm performs the following steps:

\begin{align*} 360 \amp = 1 \cdot 220 + 140 \\ 220 \amp = 1 \cdot 140 + 80 \\ 140 \amp = 1 \cdot 80 + 60 \\ 80 \amp = 1 \cdot 60 + 20 \\ 60 \amp = 3 \cdot 20 + 0 \end{align*}

The GCD is the divisor in the last line (where the remainder becomes zero).
